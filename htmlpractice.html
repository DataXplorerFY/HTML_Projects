How long does it take for food to digest? The duration for food digestion varies, on average, still, it takes about 24 to 72 hours for food to pass through the digestive system from the time of ingestion to elimination. The process of digestion begins in the mouth, where biting and the release of digestive enzymes start breaking down the food. From there, the food travels through the esophagus into the stomach, where it undergoes further mechanical and chemical digestion. The incompletely digested food also enters the small intestine, where the utmost nutrient immersion takes place.
